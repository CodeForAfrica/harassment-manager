Privacy Policy

FeedShield respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

Code for Africa is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit FeedShield or use the FeedShield tool and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Data we collect about you

In order to facilitate access to and communications about the effectiveness of the tool, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name (mandatory)
  • Your country (optional)
  • Your organisation (optional)
  • Your email address (mandatory)
  • Whether you work in journalism or related fields (optional)

How we use Your Personal Data

We will only use your personal data for the purpose for which we collected it which include thefollowing:

  • To register you as a new contact and manage your relationship with us;
  • If you register to receive updates and information on our initiatives and mentoring opportunities from Us, Your personal data will be used to send you these communications which you can opt out from at any time;
  • To improve our website, products and services, or customer relationships;
  • We may also collect information on how you use our website and services where you accept certain cookies. This may include Aggregated Data such as statistical or demographic data for any purpose. Aggregated Data will be derived from your personal data but is not considered personal data in law as this data will not directly or indirectly reveal your identity.

Data Storage

The Tool may detect personal data, including some special category data, when looking for incidences of harassment.
