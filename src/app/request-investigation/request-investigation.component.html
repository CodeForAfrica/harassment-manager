Behind the team supporting you

The iLAB at Code for Africa's African Network of Centres for Investigative Reporting (ANCIR) is the continent’s largest forensic analysis team exposing disinformation and influence operations.

The team exposes the use of coordinated inauthentic behaviour or amplified hate speech and other toxic content to subvert democratic processes or to seed polarisation, attack persons or amplify extremism. The iLAB regularly briefs key decision makers at international agencies, embassies and development agencies, and in the watchdog media. Its investigative analysis contributes to the takedown/de-platforming of entire networks of inauthentic accounts on social media.
