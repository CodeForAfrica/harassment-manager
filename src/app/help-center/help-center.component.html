Product Support

Add Comments to your report

  • Sign into SafeNet with the platform of your choice to document and report online harassment targeted at you on that platform.
  • Not sure where to start? Select from grouped comments that are categorized based on their likeliness of containing harmful content.
  • Already know what you want to include? Explore all comments for a specific timeframe.
  • Add comments and context to your report on the Create Report page.
  • After you complete your report, download or store it online. Keep it for your records or share with others.

Sharing or saving your report

  • You can export your report and save it locally by downloading a PDF, printing a copy, or saving a CSV file to review later.
  • You can also save your report as a spreadsheet on Google Drive. View, share, download, or print your report with Google Sheets on Drive.
