Product Support

Add comments to your report
  1. Sign into SafeNet with the platform of your choice to document and report online harassment targeted at you on that platform.
  2. Not sure where to start? Select from grouped comments that are categorized based on their likeliness of containing harmful content.
  3. Already know what you want to include? Explore all comments for a specific timeframe.
  4. Add comments and context to your report on the Create Report page.
  5. After you complete your report, download or store it online. Keep it for your records or share with others.
 Sharing or saving your report
  • You can export your report and save it locally by downloading a PDF, printing a copy, or saving a CSV file to review later.
  • You can also save your report as a spreadsheet on Google Drive. View, share, download, or print your report with Google Sheets on Drive. Learn More
