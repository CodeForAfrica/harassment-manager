Crisis Helpline

Receive assistance from an expert counselling service

Don't suffer in silence. The FeedShield Crisis Helpline offers virtual emergency support for people facing online harassment and abuse. Trained volunteers are available 24/7 to offer support and resources on email, WhatsApp, and Google Meet.
